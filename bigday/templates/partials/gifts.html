Gifts

Do you know about the Five Love Languages? They describe five main categories for how people give and receive love: time spent together, acts of service, words of affirmation, physical affection, and gifts. Of course everybody identifies with few love languages to varying degrees and can give and receive love in different 'languages'. This is all to say that Eli and Lucy usually give and receive love through time spent together. The best present you can give us is being at our wedding.



We're also fortunate to have everything we need to start continue our lives together. Also we live in a 500 sq ft. apartment, which is wonderful for where we're at in our lives but doesn't give us a lot of space for stuff. If we can't eat it, please don't give it to us.



We want to stress that it's absolutely not necessary to get any gifts, but we know that giving gifts can be meaningful for you so we want to provide some options!


Donate

First, it would mean a lot to us if you made a donation to one of these fabulous charities in our honor.
Elizabeth Bowers Zambia Education Fund

EBZEF empowers women, childeren, and their communities in Lumwana West, Zambia, to break the cycle of poverty through education. This charity is especially close to our hearts as it's led by Linda Bowers, Eli's great aunt, in memory of her late daughter Elizabeth.

Child's Play

Child's Play is a game industry charity dedicated to improving the lives of children with toys and games in a network of over 100 hospitals worldwide.

Oregon Food Bank

The Oregon Food Bank is working to eliminate hunger and it's root causes through public policy, nutrition and garden education, and public awareness. Lucy regularly volunteers there through her workplace, and loves their mission and the kindness of their volunteers.

Electronic Frontier Foundation

The Electronic Frontier Foundation is defending civil liberties in the digital world by championing user privacy, free expression, and innovation through impact litigation, policy analysis, grassroots activism, and technology development. As software engineers, Eli and Lucy think a lot about online privacy and maintaining a free internet, and they are hugely supportive of the EFF's efforts to defend digital privacy and freedom.

Meals on Wheels

Meals on Wheels strives to meet the social and nutritional needs of older adults. They serve delicious and nutritious meals at dozen of dining centers in Multnomah, Washington and Clark counties and volunteers deliver Meals on Wheels and friendly visits to homebound elderly. Both Eli and Lucy's moms, Marci and Melissa, have volunteered hundreds of hours with Meals on Wheels (Melissa has been driving for them for almost 12 years!).

Emerald City Pet Rescue

Emerald City Pet Rescue is a no-kill Animal shelter in Seattle which rescues abused, neglected, and homeless animals from high-volume shelters around the country. This wonderful organization was where Lucy and Eli found Luna, and they couldn't be more grateful for the work they do!
